# 🚛 TransitOps — Fleet & Transport Management System

A centralized, role-based platform that replaces spreadsheets and manual logs with real-time fleet visibility, automated state management, and enforced business rules for transport operations.

Built for Oddo Hackathon 2026


# 📌 Problem Statement

Organizations that operate vehicle fleets (vans, trucks, cars) typically manage vehicles, drivers, trips, maintenance, and expenses through disconnected spreadsheets and manual processes. This leads to:

Double-booking of vehicles or drivers
Dispatching vehicles that are under maintenance
Assigning drivers with expired licenses
Overloading vehicles beyond safe capacity
No single source of truth for operational costs or fleet utilization

TransitOps solves this by digitizing the entire fleet lifecycle — from vehicle registration to trip dispatch to maintenance to financial reporting — while enforcing real-world business rules automatically, not just providing CRUD screens.
